Adopt a Traditional Paddy Variety - Become a Seed Saviour

In-situ conservation of traditional paddy varieties, and their characterisation ensures genetic purity. We conserve 300+ traditional paddy varieties including 18 varieties from Tamil Nadu. The farms where the varieties are grown are located in three different districts, Tiruvallur, Kancheepuram and Viluppuram of Tamil Nadu.

From over 1,10,000 paddy varieties, India currently has about 6000 paddy varieties only. Maintaining their genetic purity becomes very important for conserving them for the next generation. 

You can adopt 5 paddy varieties for a total cost of Rs10,000/ season. You will:

  • Recognized as a "Seed Saviour" in our website, and presented with a certificate
  • Receive a recipe book for traditional paddy varieties and new recipes
  • Receive regular updates over email and whatsapp
  • Receive end of the season report
